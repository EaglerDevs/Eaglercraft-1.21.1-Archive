EPK files not supported!

Eagler PAK files (.epk) are not yet supported in this version.

Convert them to regular zip files by importing them into Lax1dude's EaglercraftX 1.8 client and then exporting them as .zip files that can then be read by Eaglercraft 1.21.1.

After that you can import them as regular vanilla worlds.

EPK file support will be added in later updates of the Eaglercraft 1.21.1
